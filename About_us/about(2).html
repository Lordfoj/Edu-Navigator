Our Story

Founded with Passion

Edu-Navigator was born from the vision of educators and career counselors who understood the challenges students face when choosing the right college. Launched in 2023, our platform has helped thousands of students across India find their perfect educational path.

What started as a simple college directory has grown into a comprehensive guidance platform, featuring detailed college profiles, course comparisons, and personalized recommendations.

Our commitment to transparency and accuracy ensures that every piece of information helps students make confident decisions about their future.
